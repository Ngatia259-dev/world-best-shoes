Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I find reliable authorized Nike retailers in Nairobi?

I recommend checking out Two Rivers Mall, The Hub Karen, or Sarit Centre. These places have official Nike stores and trusted retailers like Little Red.

How can I tell if the Nike shoes in a Nairobi shop are authentic?

Check the SKU number on the label inside the shoe and match it with the box. Also, look at the stitching quality and the "Swoosh" logo placement.

Are there specific times of the year for the best deals in Kenya?

Yes, watch for Black Friday in November and end-of-year sales in late December for big discounts.
