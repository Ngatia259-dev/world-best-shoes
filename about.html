About World Best Shoes

Built on faith, integrity, and decades of providing quality.

Our Story

World Best Shoes began in 1997, when our founder, Nancy Ndegwa, started selling premium footwear at Gikomba Market immediately after finishing Form Four.

Through decades of hands-on experience, she mastered the art of sourcing the finest **Grade A Ex-UK and Ex-US thrift shoes**. She realized that families in Roysambu needed a reliable place to find durable, stylish footwear for men, ladies, and children without breaking the bank.

Today, we take pride in personally selecting every pair, ensuring that our customers receive only the best "Grade A" quality that looks and feels brand new.
