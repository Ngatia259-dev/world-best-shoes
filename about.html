About World Best Shoes

Built on faith, integrity, and decades of providing quality.

Our Story

World Best Shoes began in 1997, when our founder, Nancy Ndegua, started selling shoes at Kikimba Market immediately after finishing Form Four.

Through years of hands-on experience, she learned what customers truly need — durable shoes, fair prices, and honest service.

Even after challenges like the COVID-19 period, the business stood strong and continues to serve customers in Roysambu with the same values today.

Nancy Ndegua

Founder, World Best Shoes
